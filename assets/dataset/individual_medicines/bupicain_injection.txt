bupicain injection

Therapeutic Class: PAIN ANALGESICS
Action Class: Local anaesthetic (Amides)
Chemical Class: Amide derivative
Habit Forming: No

USES
Local anesthesia (Numb tissues in a specific area)

SIDE EFFECTS
Nausea
Vomiting
Paresthesia (tingling or pricking sensation)
Slow heart rate
Decreased blood pressure
High blood pressure
Dizziness
Urinary retention

SUBSTITUTES
Levo Anawin 0.25% Injection
Anawin 0.25% Injection
Sensorcaine 0.25% Injection
Bupidural 0.25% Injection
