Choledocholithiasis

SYMPTOMS
Abdominal pain (often in the upper right quadrant)
jaundice
dark urine
pale stools
nausea
vomiting
endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography (ERCP) with stone removal
sphincterotomy
Cholecystectomy

TREATMENTS
Non-surgical treatment options: Medications to manage pain and discomfort
antibiotics
