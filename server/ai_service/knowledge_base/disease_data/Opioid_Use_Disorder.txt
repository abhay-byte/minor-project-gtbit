Opioid Use Disorder

SYMPTOMS
Compulsive use of opioids
strong desire or cravings for opioids
inability to control or reduce opioid use
withdrawal symptoms (such as nausea
muscle aches
anxiety) when attempting to stop or reduce use
tolerance to opioids
continued use despite negative consequences (such as legal issues
health problems)

TREATMENTS
Medication-assisted treatment (such as methadone
buprenorphine
naltrexone)
counseling or therapy (such as cognitive-behavioral therapy
contingency management)
support groups (such as Narcotics Anonymous)
harm reduction strategies
addressing underlying psychological or social factors
lifestyle changes
ongoing monitoring and support
