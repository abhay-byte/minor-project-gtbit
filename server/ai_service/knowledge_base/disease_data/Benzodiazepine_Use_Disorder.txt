Benzodiazepine Use Disorder

SYMPTOMS
Sedation
relaxation
reduced anxiety
drowsiness
poor coordination
memory impairment
withdrawal symptoms (such as rebound anxiety
insomnia
tremors) when attempting to stop or reduce benzodiazepine use
continued use despite negative consequences (such as relationship problems
health risks)

TREATMENTS
Gradual tapering off benzodiazepines under medical supervision
counseling or therapy (such as cognitive-behavioral therapy)
support groups
addressing underlying psychological or social factors
lifestyle changes
addressing co-occurring mental health conditions
harm reduction strategies
ongoing monitoring and support
