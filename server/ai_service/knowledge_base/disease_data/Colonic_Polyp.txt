Colonic Polyp

SYMPTOMS
Most colonic polyps do not cause symptoms. Some larger polyps or certain types may cause rectal bleeding or changes in bowel habits
such as diarrhea or constipation.

TREATMENTS
Polypectomy (removal of polyp during colonoscopy)
surveillance colonoscopy (regular monitoring for recurrence or new polyps)
lifestyle modifications (healthy diet
regular exercise
smoking cessation)
follow-up based on pathology findings
