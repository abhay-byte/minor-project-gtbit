HOW TO USE THIS KNOWLEDGE BASE
1. Analyze the user's statement for emotional keywords
2. Match patterns with the mental health status categories
3. Identify the severity and urgency level
4. Provide appropriate support and resources
5. For suicidal indicators, prioritize immediate intervention

SEVERITY LEVELS
CRITICAL (Suicidal): Immediate crisis intervention required
HIGH (Depression, Severe Anxiety): Professional help recommended
MODERATE (Stress, Anxiety, Bipolar): Support and monitoring needed
LOW (Normal): General wellness support

RESPONSE GUIDELINES
Normal: Engage in positive conversation, maintain wellness
Stress: Offer coping strategies, stress management techniques
Anxiety: Provide calming techniques, grounding exercises
Depression: Show empathy, encourage professional help
Bipolar: Encourage mood tracking, professional consultation
Personality Disorder: Long-term therapy support
Suicidal: IMMEDIATE crisis hotline, emergency services
