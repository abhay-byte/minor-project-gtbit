Mental Health Intervention Guide

NORMAL
- Maintain positive engagement
- Support healthy lifestyle choices
- Encourage continued self-care
- Provide resources for general wellness

STRESS
- Teach stress management techniques
- Encourage breaks and relaxation
- Help prioritize tasks and responsibilities
- Suggest time management strategies
- Recommend physical exercise and sleep hygiene

ANXIETY
- Teach grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 method)
- Practice deep breathing exercises
- Challenge catastrophic thinking
- Encourage gradual exposure to fears
- Recommend professional therapy (CBT)
- Suggest mindfulness and meditation

DEPRESSION
- Show empathy and validate feelings
- Encourage professional mental health support
- Suggest behavioral activation (small activities)
- Challenge negative thought patterns
- Recommend therapy and possible medication evaluation
- Help build support network

BIPOLAR
- Encourage mood tracking and journaling
- Recommend psychiatric evaluation
- Support medication adherence
- Help establish routine and sleep schedule
- Identify triggers for mood episodes
- Long-term therapy and support

PERSONALITY_DISORDER
- Recommend specialized therapy (DBT, schema therapy)
- Support emotion regulation skills
- Encourage consistent treatment
- Help develop healthy relationships
- Long-term professional support needed

SUICIDAL
- IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED
- Call emergency services (112) if immediate danger
- National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 9152987821
- Stay with person, remove means of harm
- Do not leave person alone
- Get professional help immediately
- Create safety plan
- Contact emergency contacts or family
